I want to advertise, but how?

  • First, select the page, panel and slot where you would like to see your ad and know the corresponding price from this table on this page. Please do not forget to read the notes below that table.
  • Second, read our terms and conditions as mentioned here on this page.
  • Third, if you agree with our terms and conditions, make contact with us. Contact details are mentioned here on this page
  • Fourth, upon contact fix the mode of payment and pay accordingly as soon as possible.
  • Fifth, as soon as the payment is made, send us an email or sms with the following details:
    • Payment Details including amount
    • Your Name and Contact Details
    • Details about where you would like to post your ad:
      • Page Name
      • Panel Name
      • Slot Number
  • After receiving payment, email or sms as mentioned above, your ad will be posted as per terms and conditions.

Terms and Conditions

  1. Interested parties may subscribe for as many advertisement slots as they like as per the above-mentioned price table and as per the ad space availability.
  2. Minimum duration of any subscription will be one month and maximum six months.
  3. Existing (ongoing) subscribers should renew their subscription before ten days from their subscription expiry date.
  4. Once subscribed, subscription cannot be withdrawn and hence no refund will be made to any subscriber if they wish to withdraw.
  5. It is the site owner's right to accept or decline any subscription from any party.
  6. Nudity, Vulgarity or Obscene languages or imagery are not allowed. If such contents are sent, those will not be shown on our web pages and for that no refund will be made to the faulty subscriber.
  7. Any ad will qualify for posting as soon as the required payment and other particulars as mentioned below are received.
  8. If more than one party show interest for any advertisement slot, then the party with higher price quotation will be entertained.
  9. If more than one party send payments for a same slot:
    1. Ads will be posted on our site on the following guide lines and as the site owner likes. For such site owner will not be questionable.
    2. As mentioned above, ad for higher price quotation will be posted first.
    3. Ad will be posted for the party whose payement and other required particulars as mentioned below are received first.
    4. In such a case, other party's Ad will be posted to the next slot of the same panel if it is available. In this case, no refund or compensation will be made.
    5. If no such slot in the same panel is available, Ad placement on other location or refund will be done on advertiser's advice.
    6. It is very important to note that no refund can be made to outside of Bangladesh. It can be done only within Bangladesh.
  10. We do not guarantee any flow of traffic to subscriber's site.
  11. If Our Web Server goes down temporarily for any reason, no refund or compensation will be made to the subscribers.
  12. It is very important to note that if it is required for any reason, no refund can be made to outside of Bangladesh. It can be done only within Bangladesh.
  13. Mode of payment should be fixed on telephonic or email communication. It could be bank transfer, Western Union, bCash, etc.
